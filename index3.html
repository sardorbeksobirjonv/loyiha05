About Me

PayUz Solutions

Fintech • Tashkent, UZ

About the Startup

PayUz Solutions is aiming to bridge the gap between urban banking infrastructure and rural communities in Uzbekistan. Our SMS-based payment protocol allows users with basic feature phones to conduct secure transactions without internet connectivity.

We have already piloted in 3 regions (Fergana, Namangan, Andijan) with over 10,000 active users. We are seeking investment to expand our server infrastructure and obtain a national payment license.

The Problem

  • Limited internet penetration in remote villages.
  • High cash dependency for agricultural trade.
  • Lack of digital credit history for farmers.
